**PRIVACY POLICY**

Last Updated: Febuary 13th, 2026

Service Hours ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Service Hours mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

**IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR MINORS:** This App is designed for use by high school students. If you are under 18 years of age, please review this Privacy Policy with your parent or guardian. This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect information you provide directly:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, date of birth
- **Educational Information:** Ontario Education Number (OEN), school affiliation, principal name
- **Service Hours Data:** Organization details, supervisor information, hours logged, descriptions of activities
- **Contact Information:** Phone numbers, email addresses of supervisors
- **Signatures:** Digital signatures from students, parents, and supervisors

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, operating system)
- App usage data and analytics
- IP address and general location data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Process and verify community service hours
- Send transactional emails (signature requests, verifications)
- Communicate with schools and organizations
- Comply with legal obligations
- Improve our services

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secure Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the Canada (Central) region (ca-central-1).
- We use encryption for data in transit and at rest
- Signatures are stored securely in AWS S3
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:
- Your account remains active
- Necessary to provide services
- Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

For students, service hour records may be retained for 7 years after graduation to support educational and employment verification needs.

## 6. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal information
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Withdraw consent** for data collection (may limit App functionality)
- **Request deletion** of your data
- **File a complaint** with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise these rights, contact us at sdevine.ca@gmail.com.

## 7. Parental Rights and Minor Users

If you are under 18:
- We encourage you to review this policy with a parent/guardian
- Parents/guardians may contact us to review, correct, or delete their child's information
- Parental signatures may be required for certain App functions

## 8. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your information:
- To schools/organizations you are affiliated with
- To supervisors for signature verification
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your consent

We do NOT sell your personal information.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via:
- In-app notification
- Email to registered users
- Updated "Last Updated" date
